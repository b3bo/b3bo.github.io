Where is Wild Heron located?
Wild Heron is a prestigious gated community in Panama City Beach, Florida, situated on 734 acres directly on Lake Powell, one of Northwest Florida's largest coastal dune lakes. Located at the border of Walton and Bay Counties, it's convenient to Northwest Florida Beaches International Airport (ECP) and close to both Panama City Beach attractions and the renowned 30A corridor.
What types of properties are available in Wild Heron?
Wild Heron features multiple neighborhoods with single-family homes, townhomes, and condominiums. The community offers various architectural interpretations of coastal craftsman style, from lakefront estates to low-maintenance attached residences, developed by multiple respected builders.
What are the price ranges for homes in Wild Heron?
Property prices vary by neighborhood and home type. Single-family homes typically range from interior lots to premium lakefront estates, while townhomes and condominiums offer different price points. Condos and townhomes start at $450,000 and single-family homes from $600,000 to over $2.8 million, with a median list price of $1,000,000. What amenities does Wild Heron offer?
Wild Heron residents enjoy Har-Tru Hydrocourt Tennis Courts, a community swimming pool, lakeside grill and fire pit areas, and The Boathouse at Prospect Point with kayaks and canoes. The community features miles of boardwalks and trails, dedicated parks throughout the 734-acre sanctuary, and proximity to the Greg Norman-designed Shark's Tooth Golf Club (requires separate Watersound Club membership).

Why do people choose Wild Heron?
